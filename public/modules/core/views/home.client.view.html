Our Mission

The Just Love Foundation 501c(3) believes in enhancing the opportunities of those from undeserved communities. The unskilled face a challenging future with the continued march towards automating our society. Economic equality can not be achieved through legislation, it will take a deliberate effort to mentor and equip those who are in danger of systematically dwelling under the poverty line.
